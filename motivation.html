Daily Goals

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that helps students focus by breaking work into 25-minute intervals, called pomodoros, with short breaks in between. The technique is designed to help students avoid burnout and maintain focus.

  • Intervals of study 3 an hour 1/2
  • Workout intervals 2 an hour
  • Skip a meal each day
